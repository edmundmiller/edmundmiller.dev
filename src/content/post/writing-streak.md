This was my second year reading [The Daily Stoic](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/29093292-the-daily-stoic). By June, I was still on the May 16 entry. My daily reading streak clearly needed help.

That entry described a chain method for writing. Mark each writing day on a calendar. As the chain grows, continuing it becomes its own reason to write again.

My partner was writing often for a new job and liked streaks, so I suggested that we start one together. We used my [Lego Grad Student calendar](https://brickademics.com/) to record each day. The calendar was also still on May.

This was day one. A blog post counted, but so did writing in [my slipbox](https://slipbox.edmundmiller.dev/). Paper notes in the slipbox and project documentation counted too.

The rule was broad on purpose: write something useful, then mark a physical X. We would see how long the chain lasted.
